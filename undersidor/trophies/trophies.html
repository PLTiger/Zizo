Real Madrid Trophies

Real Madrid has managed to achieve a large number of titles and sporting championships throughout its long history in football, whether at the local, European or even continental levels; Locally, the Royal Club won the Spanish Cup title nineteen times, the Spanish League title thirty-four times, and the Spanish Super Cup eleven times, while in Europe, Real Madrid won the European Cup twice, the European Champions League thirteen times, and the European Super Cup four times. , And the Spanish club's surnames were not limited to the domestic and European level only; It also extended to the continental and global levels, including the FIFA Club World Cup seven times, and the Club World Cup two times.
