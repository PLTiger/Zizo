About The Team

Real Madrid is one of the professional football clubs in Spain, based in the capital, and its players wear all-white club uniforms, which is why the club is famous for the title "Los Blancos", meaning the white color in flying, Madrid is one of the most famous and popular football clubs; As it is a large audience in Spain and in all countries of the world.



Establishing Of Real Madrid

The club was established in May 1902 and was chaired by Juan Padrós. It is noteworthy that Julian Palacios played a major role in establishing the club, as he was one of the advocates for the establishment of the club. But it was Juan Badrós who officially created it in 1902. With the spread of the game and the increasing number of fans, the club’s managers proposed holding a football competition in Madrid, and the first competition was held with the participation of five football clubs, and then the King of Spain competition was established in which the club distinguished Madrid by obtaining the title for four Times in the first six tournaments.

Spanish football experienced problems in its early stages, which had an impact on Madrid at the time, but the club's management tried to overcome these obstacles, and they enlarged their stadiums to accommodate a greater number of fans, and this led to an increase in the team's financial revenues, and the increase in fans' turnout pushed the Spanish Royal House to award the title Real, which means my property to the club, to become "Real Madrid" or "Royal Club of Madrid".

Real Madrid's Stadiums

Several stadiums have been established for Real Madrid, the most prominent of which are:

●Camp del La Industrial, which is Real Madrid's first stadium; It was inaugurated in 1912, and subsequently witnessed expansions to increase its capacity for the masses.
●Chamartin Stadium: It is located in the northern region of the capital, Madrid, and was officially inaugurated in May 1924; When Real Madrid hosted Newcastle United with a friendly match that ended with Real Madrid winning 3-2, and the club underwent restoration and expansion processes, and it was officially reopened in 1939 with a greater audience capacity than before; Where it can accommodate approximately 25 thousand spectators.
●Santiago Bernabeu Stadium: It is the club's current home stadium.
